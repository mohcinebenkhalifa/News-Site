POLITICS

Politics is the set of activities that are associated with making decisions in groups, or other forms of power relations among individuals, such as the distribution of resources or status. The branch of social science that studies politics and government is referred to as political science.

Aziz Akhannouch: Morocco is Committed to US-Africa Partnership

Rabat - The head of Morocco’s government Aziz Akhannouch has stressed the North African country’s commitment toward strengthening and supporting partnership and cooperation between Africa and the United States in various areas. Akhannouch made the statement at the US-Africa Business Summit, which is taking place in Washington between December 13 and 15. Maghreb Arabe Press (MAP) agency quoted Akhannouch as saying that strengthening ties with both the African continent and the US is at the heart of Morocco’s interests. The head of the government highlighted King Mohammed VI’s efforts to solidify relations with fellow African countries, as he had paid over 50 state visits to 30 African nations and signed over 1,000 agreements in recent years.

GCC Supreme Council Reiterates Support for Morocco’s Territorial Integrity

Rabat - The Supreme Council of the Gulf Cooperation Council for the Arab States (GCC) reiterated on Friday its support for Morocco’s sovereignty over Western Sahara. The council stressed in its closing statements “the importance of the privileged strategic partnership” between the GCC and Morocco, as well as its support for “the Moroccanness of the Sahara and the preservation of the security and stability of the Kingdom of Morocco and its territorial integrity.” The council also welcomed the adoption of UN Security Council resolution 2654, which extended the mandate of MINURSO until the end of October 2023.

King Mohammed VI Sends Message to Qatar’s Emir on Bilateral Cooperation

Rabat- King Mohammed VI sent an oral message to Emir of Qatar Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani on Thursday. Morocco’s Foreign Affairs Minister Nasser Bourita conveyed King Mohammed VI’s message to the Qatari Emir, Qatar’s news agency QNA reported. The message covers the countries’ mutual desire to boost bilateral cooperation and relations. The King’s message came after a telephone call between King Mohammed VI and Al Thani on Tuesday.

Akhannouch Represents Morocco at China-Arab Cooperation Summit

Rabat - Morocco’s Head of Government, Aziz Akhannouch, headed to Saudi Arabia Thursday to represent King Mohammed VI at the China-Arab Cooperation and Development Summit. The symposium, which opened today in Riyadh, aims to boost economic, scientific, and technical cooperation between China and Arab countries. During the meeting, high-level officials from China and Arab countries will work to define the road for future cooperation, as well as to reinforce their consensus on major issues - including global governance, development, security, and dialogue. The participants will also discuss the cooperation mechanism to further expertise exchange between the two sides to address the global challenges and changes.

French Spokesperson Confirms FM Catherine Colonna’s Visit to Morocco in December

Rabat - French Minister of Foreign Affairs Catherine Colonna will visit Morocco as part of an official trip from December 15-16. A spokeswoman from the French government confirmed the news to French news agency AFP on Thursday. “The minister will arrive on the evening of the 15th and will have a meeting the next day with her counterpart Nasser Bourita… They will discuss all the subjects of the bilateral relationship.” between the two countries- particularly the visa issue, AFP quoted the spokeswoman as saying..
